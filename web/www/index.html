Speedreading & Spaced Repetition

The keys to efficient learning.

Don't waste time reading notes

Did you know that on average, you read at 180 words per minute on a screen? That's 40% slower than you'd read a book! StudyPiggy has a speedreader built in that allows you to read at 1000 words per minute, even if you've never tried speedreading before.

Don't review more often than necessary

You may have heard of the learning curve, but have you every heard of the forgetting curve? StudyPiggy tells you exactly when to review your notes, to compensate for the forgetting curve. Never study more often that you need to, and maintain maximum retnetion.

When a note appears green in your sidebar, it's time to review. Just open up the speedreader, blast through your notes, and wait until your next note turns green.

Not sure when you should study?

Don't worry, we'll take of that too! Just download our companion app, and we'll take care of everything for you. When you need to review a certain, we'll send you a push notification. Just swipe open your notification, read your note, and you're good to go. It takes less time to review a note than it does to send a text.
